Submission/Review Process

Before Submission

Before you can submit your paper, you must first create an IJTISI login. Do this by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the navigation bar at the top right of your screen.

Once you have done this, you will be given access to submit your works to the Editor.


Initial Submission

When you submit your paper to the Editor, they will pre-screen your work and decide on whom will be reviewing it. This decision is based upon the subject matter of your paper. For example, if it is a piece written on the subject of Cybersecurity, the Editor will assign your paper to be reviewed by a member of the Review Board that specializes in that field.
