IJTISI - Exploring the Future

International Journal of Technology, Information Systems and Innovation


A New Journal!

IJTISI is a peer reviewed, academic journal that features scholarly articles from multiple disciplines with an international audience to explore new frontiers in information technology, information systems education, and innovation. Particularly noteworthy papers from the annual ISECON conference may be selected for inclusion in the journal. The Journal welcomes the submission of original articles that may be of interest to its audience. Furthermore, it encourages work in progress as research notes and student projects. Finally, the Journal features book reviews on new books in related subject areas.
