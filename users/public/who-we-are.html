INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF TECHNOLOGY, INFORMATION SYSTEMS, & INNOVATION

Founding Editor: Ali Kamali, Ph.D. (Missouri Western State University)

The International Journal of Technology, Information Systems, & Innovation (IJTISI) is a peer reviewed, academic journal that is sponsored by the Foundation for Information Technology Education (FITE). The journal is multidisciplinary with a focus on international audience. IJTISI reflects current IT related research and projects outcomes that are produced by those who are exploring interesting questions about information systems education. IJTISI promotes and publicizes new developments and findings in the field of IT, helps research for the purpose of scientific progress, and supplies a scientific platform for a dialog about IT related topics. As an academic journal, IJTISI claims to be a mode of communication among researchers and scholars whose contributions add to a cumulative body of scientific knowledge pertaining also to the future of IT. Contents that intend to address the international audience, and perhaps international IT issues, are most welcomed. They may include but are not limited to:

  • Cloud-based Systems
