Submission Guidelines

Submissions must adhere to the following guidelines:

Since IJTISI adheres to a blind review process, authors must refrains from including any identifying information in the version of the paper submitted for review. However, the following information must be provided via the submission page.

  • Author(s)
  • Email address(es)
  • Institutional or company affiliation(s)
  • Corresponding Author
  • Paper title
  • Paper abstract
  • Paper keywords. The paper type should also be indicated at the beginning of the keywords blank on the submission form.

THE CONTENTS

  1. Papers submitted for review (as a computer file) should include the paper’s title at its beginning (first page), but should not identify the author(s). No other identifying information in the paper should disclose the identity of the author(s).
  2. Papers should not exceed twenty (20) pages, including all appendices. Most papers are ten (10) pages or less, using the font and margin settings specified in these submission guidelines.
  3. All text contained in the paper should be in Verdana type with the paper title in 18-point font and the body of the text in 10-point Verdana. Page margins are in inches: 1.2″ top and bottom, 1.0″ left and right margins. Text should appear in two columns that are 3.1 inches wide (each) with a gap of 0.3 inches between the columns. Paragraphs should be set to alignment justified and single-spaced.
  4. All pages are to be U.S. letter-size (8.5″ x 11″).
  5. Any tables or figures that exceed the width of the columns should be included in an appendix.
  6. Appendices may be formatted in any way the author(s) deem necessary, with the exception that all pages must adhere to the standard page margins on an 8.5″ x 11″ page as listed above. Any rotated pages in landscape orientation in the appendices should be rotated ninety (90) degrees to the left (commonly referred to as “counter-clockwise” or “anti-clockwise” rotation).
  7. References should be listed alphabetically at the end of the paper according to APA style guidelines. Parentheses should be used within the body of the paper to offset citations.
  8. All submissions must be provided in one single file (e.g., no multi-file submissions will be accepted). The file must be in one of the following file formats: Adobe PDF, Microsoft Word DOC, or Microsoft Word DOCX. All figures that are contained within Microsoft Word files must allow the file to be easily converted to PDF format from within Word. Authors are advised to limit figures to either JPG or GIF format for this purpose when embedding the image within the document’s pages.
